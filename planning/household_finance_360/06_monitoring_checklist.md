# Monitoring Checklist

## Monthly

- Update latest income and expense totals
- Reconcile actual spending against budget
- Update end-of-month balances
- Update net worth snapshot
- Check savings rate
- Check progress against:
  - emergency fund
  - house expansion
  - education
  - retirement
- Note any anomalies or one-off expenses

## Quarterly

- Review category drift in spending
- Review upcoming large expenses
- Review goal timelines and funding gaps
- Review investment allocation by goal
- Reduce risk for near-term goals if needed
- Review debt balance and interest costs

## Annually

- Complete annual review template
- Refresh income assumptions
- Refresh expected project costs
- Review retirement targets
- Review insurance adequacy
- Review beneficiaries and estate documents
- Review tax wrappers and contribution strategy
